# Octave Band
## Settings
- Octave Ratio
    - Exponent base used to define octave ratio
    - Default, Base 10
- Bandwidth Fraction
    - Fraction specifying widths of fractional-octave bands
    - Default, 1/3
- Min Center Freq
    - Minimum center frequency for octaves in filter bank
    - Default, 20.0 Hz
- Max Center Freq
    - Maximum center frequency for octaves in filter bank
    - Default, 20000.00 Hz
- Weighting Scheme
    - Options, None, Z, A, C
    - Default, None
- Stats
    - Statistics to collect for octave bands
    - Maximum
    - Minimum
    - Average
    - Peak
    - Peak to Peak
    - Root Mean Square
    - Signal Energy
    - Signal Power
